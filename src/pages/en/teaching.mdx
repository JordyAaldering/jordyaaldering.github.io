# Courses

<Timeline>
  <TimelineItem text="Green Software">
    In 2026 we developed a brand-new course on *Green Software*.
    I contributed to the course design, including the exercises and group project, and supervised students during the assignments and project work.
    I also delivered my first lecture as part of this course.
  </TimelineItem>

  <TimelineItem text="Advanced Programming">
    Since 2023 I have been involved in the *Advanced Programming* course.
    I primarily teach tutorial sessions and provide feedback on student assignments.
    I also introduced a new language feature, *Type Patterns*, which was incorporated into the course material.
  </TimelineItem>

  <TimelineItem text="Compiler Construction">
    In 2023 and 2024 I contributed to the *Compiler Construction* course.

  </TimelineItem>
